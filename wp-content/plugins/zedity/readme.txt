== Installation ==

**From your WordPress dashboard:**

1. Click on "Plugins" -> "Add new";

2. Type Zedity in the search bar;

3. Click on "Install now";

4. Click on "Activate Plugin".

You can also find step by step instructions in this [installation tutorial](https://zedity.com/blog/plugin-installation/)

TIP: after downloading the plugin, can't you find the .zip file? That may happen if your browser is set to unzip it automatically (Safari usually does that). In that case, you can right click on the unzipped folder and compress it: the .zip file is ready to be installed.

== Frequently asked questions ==

**Q: Why should I use Zedity?**  
A: Zedity enables you to do things that cannot be done with the WordPress visual editor, creating your posts and pages, very easily, quickly. For example, you position any box (videos, images, text, audio, etc.) where you want, add effects and many more things without having to write a single line of HTML, Javascript or CSS code!

**Q: How can I start creating content with Zedity?**  
A: After installing and activating the plugin, the "Z" icon (button) appears among the others icons in the WordPress visual editor section. Just click on the "Z" button to open Zedity, and you can start adding your content right away. Once you're done, save your content ("Content" -> "Save") to exit the Zedity Editor: your content is now ready to be either previewed or published. You can add Zedity content anywhere in your pages or posts, as many times as you want.

**Q: How come audio and video boxes are not playing after I exit the Zedity Editor?**  
A: No worries, that is the normal behavior in WordPress: they can be played back when you either preview or publish your content.

**Q: How can I update the Zedity content after saving it?**  
A: From the standard WordPress Visual editor: 1) click on the Zedity content that you want to update; 2) click on the Zedity icon that appears on top of the content.

**Q: What video services are supported?**  
A: YouTube and Vimeo available for both Free and Premium version, which supports [20+ audio and video services](https://zedity.com/plugin/wpfeatures)

**Q: Do you have tutorials?**  
A: Yes, here you can find useful information, video and tutorials [Zedity Blog](https://zedity.com/blog)

For any other question, please feel free to [contact us](https://zedity.com/support).

== Screenshots ==

1. By clicking on the Z icon, which appears among the other icons of the standard Visual WordPress Editor, the Zedity editor opens up.
2. To get started, click on the "Add box" menu and then, for example, click on "Image" to add an image, on "Text" to add text, etc.
3. Add other boxes, drag them around to create the desired design and, eventually, click on the Save button to save your content.
4. From the WordPress dashboard, you can control settings for the default content size of your new contents, the web fonts you intend to use, the default content responsiveness (Premium feature), and many others to customize Zedity the way you want.
